# Bigger Bets for AI-Native Products

## Classification
Framework, not a runtime skill.

## Why it moved out of /skills
This content is strategically useful, but it is not usually invoked as a just-in-time execution behavior. It is a portfolio-allocation and strategy framework.

## When to use this framework
- A leadership team is deciding roadmap mix
- A product org is over-indexed on micro-optimization
- A team needs to evaluate whether AI-native product dynamics justify larger bets

## Core idea
If AI is central to product value and that value is compounding rapidly, small optimizations should not dominate the portfolio. The faster future value expands, the more important it becomes to discover and place larger bets earlier.

## Use this framework to assess
- whether AI is central or peripheral to product value
- whether future value expansion materially changes the opportunity set
- whether the current roadmap is overly skewed to small measurable wins
- which larger strategic bets are being underfunded

## Recommended output
Return:
- AI centrality assessment
- current portfolio mix
- recommended future mix
- candidate large bets
- rationale
- key risks
