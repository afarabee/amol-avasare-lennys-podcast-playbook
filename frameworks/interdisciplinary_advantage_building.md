# Interdisciplinary Advantage Building

## Classification
Framework, not a runtime skill.

## Why it moved out of /skills
This is highly useful for coaching and career design, but it is more diagnostic and developmental than triggerable as a runtime procedure.

## When to use this framework
- A person wants to future-proof their role
- A leader is coaching for differentiated leverage
- A team wants to understand which hybrid profiles matter most

## Core idea
Generic competence is less defensible than distinctive combinations. Deep spikes and cross-domain strengths become more valuable as roles blur unevenly.

## Use this framework to assess
- strongest spikes
- adjacent domains with leverage potential
- unfair-advantage combinations
- where those combinations matter most

## Recommended output
Return:
- current strengths
- possible hybrid combinations
- best unfair-advantage paths
- recommended development moves
- where the combination will matter most
