# Cold Outreach for High-Value Roles

## Purpose
Use this skill to create high-conviction outreach when a role, collaboration, or opportunity may not be formally posted.

## Use this skill when
- The target opportunity is high value
- Standard channels are weak or crowded
- The sender has a specific point of view to offer
- The goal is to get a conversation, not tell a full story

## Do not use this skill when
- The opportunity is low priority
- The user has no clear angle or value proposition
- A formal process is clearly required and sufficient

## Core principles
1. Opening matters first; the message must get opened.
2. Channel choice matters.
3. Keep the message short.
4. State who you are, why you are relevant, and why the conversation matters now.
5. Follow up persistently if the opportunity is important.

## Workflow
1. Define the target and why standard channels may fail.
2. Identify the specific insight or value proposition.
3. Select the least crowded viable channel.
4. Draft a short note with:
   - signal of familiarity
   - clear point of view
   - why you are relevant
   - direct call to talk
5. Plan follow-ups.

## Output format
Return:
- target
- key angle
- outreach draft
- follow-up sequence
- risks and likely objections
