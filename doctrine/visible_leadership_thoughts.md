# Visible Leadership Thoughts

## Classification
Doctrine, not a skill.

## Why it moved out of /skills
This is a cultural operating principle. It can inform communication system design, but it is not usually selected as a just-in-time skill.

## Core doctrine
Leaders should publish thought process, not just decisions. Visible reasoning scales judgment, accelerates onboarding, and improves organizational context for both humans and AI systems.

## Use this doctrine when designing
- internal communication patterns
- onboarding systems
- leadership transparency norms
- AI context-sharing practices

## Implication
Judgment becomes more portable when it is made visible. Context-sharing is not overhead; it is a strategic operating asset.
